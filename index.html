About Me

Hello! I’m Julia Yates, an author of historical romance books. This is my space to share updates, new releases, and my journey. Julia Yates is a pen name to dabble in penning tales. Currently exploring historical romance and magical realism as a genre! In her free time, Julia likes reading, watching video essays on internet drama and kdramas.

Books

Gone Without Goodbyes (A Fated Love #1)

A Regency Romance

In the opulent yet rigidly stratified world of Regency England, Gone Without Goodbyes tells the poignant story of Alexander, the young heir to a dukedom, and Amelia, the rector's kind and resilient daughter. Their love blossoms against societal expectations and familial obligations, challenging them to defy conventions. "Gone Without Goodbyes" explores love, especially one that comes too late.

Bound By Betrayal (A Fated Love #2)

A Regency Romance

In the serene countryside of Regency England, Henry Fitzroy’s life appears perfect, marked by a successful estate and a seemingly blissful marriage to Lady Frances. After a tragic accident and revelations of betrayal, Henry finds solace with Lady Georgiana Churchill. Their love, born from heartbreak, promises redemption but tests duty and honor.
